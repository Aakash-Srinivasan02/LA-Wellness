Recovery Resources in Orange County

Here are some helpful resources for individuals in recovery, their families, and loved ones in Orange County, California. These include hotlines, support groups, treatment centers, and educational materials.

Hotlines & Crisis Support

Orange County Crisis Line

24/7 crisis intervention and suicide prevention

Phone: (949) 645-1122

Website: occrisiscare.org

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

24/7 support for anyone in crisis

Phone: 988

Website: 988lifeline.org

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA)

National helpline for treatment referral

Phone: 1-800-662-HELP (4357)

Website: samhsa.gov/find-help/national-helpline
