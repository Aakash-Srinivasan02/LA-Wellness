About Our Recovery Center

Our recovery center is dedicated to providing compassionate, effective care for individuals struggling with addiction and mental health challenges. We believe in a holistic approach that addresses the whole person - body, mind, and spirit.

Our Mission

To create a safe, supportive environment where individuals can heal, grow, and rebuild their lives free from addiction.

Color-Coded Houses

Each of our sober living houses is color-coded to help residents identify their home and foster a sense of community:

  • Red House - Focus on strength and energy
  • Blue House - Emphasis on calm and trust
  • Orange House - Creativity and enthusiasm
  • Pink House - Compassion and nurturing
  • Green House - Growth and renewal

420-Friendly Approach

We understand that cannabis can play a role in harm reduction for some individuals. Our programs provide education and support for those who choose to incorporate responsible cannabis use into their recovery journey.
